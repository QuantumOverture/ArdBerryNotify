ArdBerry Notify





- What is this? -

It is a website that allows me to integrate a bunch of API requests and make it easy for my Raspberry PI(+Arduino) to get data.

- What are the Raspberry Pi and Arduino doing? -

Turning LEDs on and off or changing their color in response to different changes in data in order to notify me of new messages,weather data, and class times.
